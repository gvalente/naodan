NAODAN CHARTERING INC.

Established in June of 1992, NAODAN Chartering Inc. is located in the United States Pacific Northwest on Mercer Island in Washington state. For nearly 30 years, NAODAN has earned a reputation as a trusted partner to timber and bulk cargo shippers worldwide, facilitating successful global ocean transportation solutions.

Seeking to provide our customers with greater market intelligence and global reach, in 2019, NAODAN entered into an agreement with Maersk Broker of Copenhagen Denmark to be acquired within their group of companies. Maersk Broker, founded in 1914 by A.P Moller, is represented in 15 countries, with 18 offices and more than 250 brokers and other staff working to ensure that you receive the most appropriate and accurate business intelligence required for your specific business proposition.
